27 August 2025

SierraKomodo updated:

  • Email reply chains no longer keep prefixing additional 'Re: ' if the prefix already exists. No more 'Re: Re: Re: Re: Re: Re: Re: Re: Re: Re: Re: Re: Re: Re: Re: Re: Re: Re: Re: Re: Re: Re: Re: Re: Re: Re: Re: No Subject' in your inbox!
