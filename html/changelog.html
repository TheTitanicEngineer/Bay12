27 August 2025

SierraKomodo updated:

  • Email reply chains no longer keep prefixing additional 'Re: ' if the prefix already exists. No more 'Re: Re: Re: Re: Re: Re: Re: Re: Re: Re: Re: Re: Re: Re: Re: Re: Re: Re: Re: Re: Re: Re: Re: Re: Re: Re: Re: No Subject' in your inbox!
  • Emails can now be forwarded!
  • Added popup tooltips to the icon buttons on email message listings, describing what each button does. (Delete, Reply, Forward, Open).
  • Supply orders for ammunition are now separated between boxes of bullets and empty magazines. Supply point costs adjusted to match. The ammoboxes are also the new ones that I might have forgotten to actually implement. Includes some QOL features such as clicking on empty magazines with a box to speed-fill the mag, or a box with a mag to speed-empty it.
